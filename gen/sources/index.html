Family Documents

My father inherited several of the family genealogy sources. These have been a great help in my quest for finding my ancestors. My mother also did genealogy work during the 1970s and gathered several documents pertaining to the lives of my father's ancestors. These pages are digital images of these sources along with any known information pertaining to the individuals who the sources are about.
