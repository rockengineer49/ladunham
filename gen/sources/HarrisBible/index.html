GENERAL SUPERIENTENDENT'S OFFICE.

Paris, Ill., Nov. 20th 1874

Mr. E. T. HARRIS is authorized to act as Master Mechanic. He will have supervision over all engines and mechinery belonging to this Company, until further orders.

Engineers and others in his department will report to him, and respect his orders.

B.F. MATTHIAS Gen'l Supt.
