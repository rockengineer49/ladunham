Charles William Harris

Entry for Charles in Michigan Volunteers, 1861-1865:

Harris, Charles W., Jackson. Enlisted in company I, Fourth Cavalry, as Corporal, Aug. 11, 1862, at Jackson, for 3 years, age 23. Mustered Aug. 29, 1862. Sergeant. Died of disease at Jackson, Mich., March 1, 1864.[1]

Melville Harris

Entry for Melville in Michigan Volunteers, 1861-1865:

Harris, Melville C., or Melvin, Springport. Enlisted in company I, Fourth Cavalry, Aug. 11, 1862, at Jackson, for 3 years, age 18. Mustered Aug. 28, 1862. Died of disease at Murfreesboro, Tenn., Jan 16, 1863.[1]

Endnotes

[1]Michigan. Adjutant-General's Department, and George H Turner. Record of Service of Michigan Volunteers In the Civil War, 1861-1865. [Kalamazoo, Mich.: Ihling bros. & Everard, printers, 190.
